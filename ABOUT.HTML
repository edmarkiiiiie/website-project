About Jumbo Siomai

Jumbo Siomai has been serving delicious, flavorful, and homemade Siomai. With a commitment to high-quality ingredients and customer satisfaction, we have expanded rapidly across Quezon City, with numerous resellers and branches proudly offering Jumbo Siomai's Big Taste, Jumbolicious experience.

SEPTEMBER 2024 - Founding of Jumbo Siomai

Jumbo Siomai was founded with the goal of offering affordable, tasty, and high-quality siomai to Filipino families. The business quickly gained a loyal following.

NOVEMBER 2024- First Resellers Join

Our first resellers joined the business, helping Jumbo Siomai reach more people across the Quezon City. The demand for our products grew exponentially.

DECEMBER 2024- Television Feature

Jumbo Siomai made its television debut in December 2024 as a featured segment on the popular show, "Eat Bulaga." This significant exposure marked a turning point for our brand, propelling Jumbo Siomai towards widespread recognition and popularity.

JANUARY 2025 - Expansion

Jumbo Siomai has expanded across Quezon City, with multiple branches and resellers helping us serve even more customers in new barangays and cities

Resellers

Currently, Jumbo Siomai has over 30 resellers across Quezon City who are proud to sell our products and share the joy of Jumbo Siomai with others.

Branches

We now have over 20 branches across the City, providing easy access to our delicious and affordable siomai!
